## The Central Finding

> LUXVAR's computationally recoverable structure is **morphemic-protocolic**,
> grounded in sub-character phonotactic patterns.
> Protocol encodes information beyond explicit morpheme presence (MASK-001).
> Frequency labels and semantic axes are not computationally encoded.
> The gap between "recognizable structure" and "generative grammar" remains open.
